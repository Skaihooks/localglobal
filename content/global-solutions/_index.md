Solutions that are independent of place. These are frameworks, technologies, institutional designs, and interventions whose viability rests on physics, thermodynamics, game theory, or structural logic rather than on the specifics of any one geography. Where [Crises](/crises/) catalogues what is going wrong, this section examines what can be done about it at a level of generality that transcends any single region.
